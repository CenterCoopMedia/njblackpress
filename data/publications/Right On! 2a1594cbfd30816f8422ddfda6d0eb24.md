# Right On!

Year founded: 1971
Owners/Publishers: D.S. Magazines, Inc. (originally Laufer Publishing Company; founded by Judy Wieder)
Location (City): Cresskill
Format: Magazine
Medium/Distribution method (e.g. Print, Digital): Print
Frequency of publication: Monthly
Primary focus/Content areas: Music, Hip hop culture, Teen entertainment
Languages published: English
HMerge:About: First full-fledged entertainment magazine for the Black youth market in the United States. Launched to promote the Jackson 5. First publication to cover hip-hop music in the 1980s. Mentioned in The Notorious B.I.G.'s "Juicy" (1994).
Key staff members: Cynthia Horner (Editor-in-Chief from 1976); current owner of Right On! Media Holdings, LLC
