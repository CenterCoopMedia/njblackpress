# Gospel Today Magazine

Year founded: 1989
Owners/Publishers: Dr. Teresa Hairston, Founder & Publisher Emeritus
Location (City): Fort Lee (Note: Originally based in New York City, later relocated to Fayetteville, Georgia)
Format: Magazine
Medium/Distribution method (e.g. Print, Digital): Print (later digital)
Frequency of publication: Bimonthly
Primary focus/Content areas: Gospel music, Music, Religion, Book reviews, Video reviews
Languages published: English
HMerge:About: Longest-running and most widely-distributed Urban Christian Lifestyle magazine in history. Peak circulation of 240,000. Started as "Gospel SCORE" 4-page newsletter in 1989, renamed to Gospel Today in 1994. Ceased operations around 2012-2013.
Key staff members: Dr. Teresa Hairston (Founder); Roland T. Hairston II (Co-publisher 2011-2012)
