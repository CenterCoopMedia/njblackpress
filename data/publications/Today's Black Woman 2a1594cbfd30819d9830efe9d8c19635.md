# Today's Black Woman

Year founded: 1994
Owners/Publishers: John Blassingame; New Day Associates, Inc.
Location (City): Paramus (later Monroe, NJ)
Format: Magazine
Medium/Distribution method (e.g. Print, Digital): Print
Frequency of publication: Bi-monthly (6 issues per year; originally nine times a year)
Primary focus/Content areas: Women, General interest, Beauty, Fashion, Careers, Economics
Languages published: English
HMerge:About: Circulation of 200,000 (as of 2005). March 2009 issue featured First Lady Michelle Obama and was later transferred to the Obama Presidential Library. Part of New Day Associates portfolio alongside Black Men Magazine and Hype Hair.
Key staff members: Kate Ferguson (Editor in Chief)
