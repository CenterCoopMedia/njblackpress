# Black Caucus of ALA Newsletter

Year founded: 1974
Owners/Publishers: Black Caucus of the American Library Association (BCALA)
Location (City): Chicago/National (BCALA founded 1970, newsletter began 1974)
Format: Newsletter
Medium/Distribution method (e.g. Print, Digital): Print
Frequency of publication: Semi-annually (twice yearly)
Primary focus/Content areas: Libraries, Education, African American librarians
Languages published: English
HMerge:About: Primary source of professional information for African American librarians. Featured BCALA logo (books forming a closed fist for solidarity) since Volume 1. Archives held at University of Illinois Library ALA Archives.
Key staff members: Dr. George C. Grant (Editor 1983-late 1980s); Various editors serving 1-3 year terms
