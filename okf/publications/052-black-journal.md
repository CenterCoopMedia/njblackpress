## Historical notes

No digitized archive or detailed historical record was found for the Black Journal of Jersey City, NJ (1979). The publication does not appear in the Library of Congress newspaper directory, the Wikipedia list of African American newspapers in New Jersey, the Newark Public Library microfilm inventory, or the New Jersey State Library's digitized newspaper index. It should not be confused with the nationally known PBS television program 'Black Journal' (1968-1977) hosted by Tony Brown, which later became 'Tony Brown's Journal.' The Jersey City Free Public Library's local history collections and the New Jersey Historical Society in Newark may hold physical records. The publication was likely a small-circulation community newspaper serving Jersey City's Black population during the late 1970s.
