## Historical notes

CCC camp newspaper. Associated with CCC Camp S-60 (Company 1284-C) at Penn State Forest, 13 miles south of Chatsworth, established October 30, 1933. No digitized copies found online. CCC camps in New Jersey were fully segregated by July 1935 per order of CCC Director Robert Fechner. Camp newspapers were a common feature of CCC camps, with an estimated 60-70 percent of camps producing their own publications. Further research recommended at the Center for Research Libraries (main repository for CCC camp newspapers) and National Archives Record Group 35.
