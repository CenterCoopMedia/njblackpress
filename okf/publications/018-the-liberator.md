## Historical notes

No specific records were found for a publication called The Liberator published in Paterson, NJ around 1950 in the African American press context. This title should not be confused with William Lloyd Garrison's famous abolitionist newspaper The Liberator (Boston, 1831-1865), nor with the 1960s New York-based Liberator magazine (1961-1971) associated with the Black liberation movement. The Paterson Liberator may have been a small community newspaper serving the city's growing African American population during the postwar era.
