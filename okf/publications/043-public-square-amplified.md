## Mission statement

PSA positions journalism as a civic tool and creates a pipeline for a new cadre of local journalists of color representing their geographic and ethnic communities.

## Key staff

Josie Gonsalves - Founder and Executive Director
Renee Johnston - Digital Administrator.

## Historical notes

Public Square Amplified (PSA) is a grassroots local nonprofit newsroom founded in 2021 by a team of Black women with over 40 years of combined experience in nonprofit, journalism, and community organizing sectors. Headquartered in Newark, PSA operates as a cooperative newsroom that positions journalism as a civic tool to amplify local voices. The organization receives provisional membership and fiscal sponsorship under the Institute for Nonprofit News and subscribes to its standards of editorial independence.
