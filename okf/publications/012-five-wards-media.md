## Key staff

Brit Harley, Director and Co-Founder.

## Historical notes

Five Wards Media grew out of the Newark News & Story Collaborative, co-founded by Brit Harley, a Newark native and former WBGO 88.3fm community engagement reporter. The initiative launched during a 2021 JSK Community Impact Fellowship, with backing from the Geraldine R. Dodge Foundation and the Victoria Foundation. The outlet was part of the first cohort of Tiny News Collective members in 2021 and has trained over 20 Newark residents as reporting fellows through its community media incubator.
