## Historical notes

This is NOT a newspaper. It is a digital library collection hosted by Rutgers University Libraries at collections.libraries.rutgers.edu/newark-black-newspapers. The collection contains four newspapers published under the leadership of Amiri Baraka (Leroi Jones) between 1968 and 1979 during Newark's post-rebellion period: (1) Black Newark, Vol. 1 No. 1-3, April-November 1968; (2) Black News, published by Black People of Newark, Vol. 1 No. 4, 1969; (3) Black New Ark, published by Committee for Unified Newark, Vol. 1 No. 5 (April 1972) - Vol. 2 No. 14 (Dec. 1973), incomplete; (4) Unity and Struggle, published by Committee for Unified Newark, Vol. 3 No. 1 (Jan/Feb 1974) - Vol. 8 No. 1/2 (Feb. 1979), incomplete. The collection is digitized from holdings at the Rutgers Dana Library and is fully searchable. These newspapers served as tools for activism, organizing, and advocating for Black liberation and political and economic agency. This entry should be reclassified as a 'collection' rather than a standalone newspaper.
