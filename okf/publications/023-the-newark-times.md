## Historical notes

The Newark Times is a digital publication dedicated to sharing the perspectives and narratives of Newark's history, emergence, and triumphs with dignity, honesty, and candor. The outlet covers Newark community news including economic development, arts events, and nonprofit investments in the city. It operates via thenewarktimes.com as part of the broader ecosystem of Newark-focused community media.
