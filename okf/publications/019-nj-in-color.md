## Mission statement

NJ In Color is a platform designed to introduce you to all of the people, places, and personalities that make North Jersey so special.

## Key staff

Tevra Francis, Publisher.

## Historical notes

NJ in Color is a digital publication based in Leonia, New Jersey, focused on connecting to the diversity of North Jersey. The outlet covers lifestyle, events, philanthropy, and news across Northern NJ communities including Bergen County, Englewood, Teaneck, Hackensack, Newark, Montclair, Paterson, Jersey City, and surrounding areas. The publication also maintains a New Jersey Black & POC Business Directory.
