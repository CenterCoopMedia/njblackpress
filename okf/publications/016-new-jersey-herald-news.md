## Mission statement

Described as 'Owned and published by New Jersey Negroes,' serving the African American community of Newark and Essex County.

## Key staff

Previous editor (s): Oliver W. Brown. Supersedes: Absorbed: Newark Herald. Merged with: Advance to form: Herald-Advance.

## Historical notes

Published by the New Jersey Herald News Publishing Co. beginning May 1938, ceasing around 1966. Weekly English-language newspaper edited by Oliver W. Brown (O.W. Brown). Succeeded The Newark Herald (1928-1939). Earliest description based on Vol. 1, No. 3 (May 21, 1938). Later succeeded by the Advance (Newark, 1965-1966) and Herald-Advance (Newark, 1966-). Digitized issues from 1938 available via Internet Archive. Also available through Newark Public Library Digital Archive.
