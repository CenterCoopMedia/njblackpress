## Historical notes

Faithfully Magazine was founded in 2016 by religion writer Nicola A. Menzie and is published by Faithfully Media, LLC, based at 397 Hillside Avenue, Hillside, NJ. The publication is a fresh, bold news and culture outlet centering on Christian communities of color, and has been featured in The Washington Post, Christianity Today, Fortune Magazine, WNYC, and other major outlets. It publishes exclusive Q&As, features, and commentaries reflecting influential voices in U.S. Christianity.
