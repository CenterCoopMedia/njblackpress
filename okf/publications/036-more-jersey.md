## Mission statement

More Jersey is a community media startup with a statewide footprint, dedicated to connecting individuals with local businesses and organizations, informing communities of activities to enhance their lives, and inspiring NJ residents to take action.

## Historical notes

More Jersey is a community media startup founded by Joe Campbell, committed to producing content, merchandise, and events to inform, connect, and inspire New Jersey residents. The outlet serves as a statewide one-stop resource covering government, community, arts and culture, finance, and events spanning the entire state. Campbell has noted the company's focus on the up-trend in audio and video-based content, including developing short-form video series to celebrate the New Jersey experience.
