## Historical notes

NJ Urban News is a BIPOC-led digital newsroom co-founded by CEO Penda Howell, headquartered at 625 Broad Street in Newark, New Jersey. The publication is committed to covering stories often overlooked by mainstream media that impact the 1.1 million people making up New Jersey's Black community. It is recognized as a Black-owned media outlet by the African American Chamber of Commerce of New Jersey.
