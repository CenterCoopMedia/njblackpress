## Historical notes

Ark Republic is a member-funded media outlet founded in 2017 and based at 142 Lafayette St, Newark, NJ. Described as a revolutionary, member-fueled media company, it is a collective of journalists, scholars, artists, and activists committed to telling rich and robust narratives about the Black experience globally. Notable recognitions include being named a Company to Watch by NYU Entrepreneurial Institute (2018), Comcast RISE recipient (2020), Newark Creative Catalyst (2021), and joining CUNY's Black Media Project inaugural cohort (2022).
