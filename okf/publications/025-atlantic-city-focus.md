## Historical notes

Atlantic City Focus is a hyper-local news platform founded by Mark Tyler, who holds a BA in Journalism from Columbia Union College (now Washington Adventist University) and previously interned at The Press of Atlantic City. The publication covers Atlantic City's arts, culture, and entertainment through an African American lens. It has participated in collaborative journalism efforts overseen by the Center for Cooperative Media at Montclair State University.
