## Historical notes

CCC camp newspaper. No CCC camp directly listed for Glen Ridge in the CCC Legacy database; may have been associated with a nearby Essex County camp. CCC camps in New Jersey were segregated by 1935. No digitized copies found online. Further research recommended at the Center for Research Libraries, National Archives Record Group 35, and the book 'The CCC Chronicles: Camp Newspapers of the Civilian Conservation Corps, 1933-1942' by Alfred Emile Cornebise (chapter 8 covers African American experience).
