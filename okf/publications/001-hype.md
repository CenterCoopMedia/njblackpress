## Historical notes

Hype was a music magazine published out of River Edge, NJ, connected to the Word Up! publishing ecosystem. It was part of the Faces Magazines / Enoble Media family of teen entertainment publications based in the Paramus/River Edge area of Bergen County, NJ. The magazine focused on hip-hop and R&B music content for teen audiences. Note: A separate, unrelated modern publication called 'The Hype Magazine' exists today as an urban culture outlet but has no direct connection to the 1990s River Edge publication. No digitized archive of the original Hype magazine was found online.
