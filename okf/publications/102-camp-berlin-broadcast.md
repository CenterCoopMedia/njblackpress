## Historical notes

CCC camp newspaper. Associated with CCC Camp SP-9 (Company 1275-C), located 1 mile west of Berlin, NJ (Camden County), established October 30, 1933. No digitized copies found online. CCC camps in New Jersey were segregated by 1935 per Director Fechner's order. Further research recommended at the Center for Research Libraries and National Archives Record Group 35.
