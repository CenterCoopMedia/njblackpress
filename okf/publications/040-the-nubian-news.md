## Historical notes

The Nubian News was founded circa 1988-1989 by publisher Kamau Kujichagulia and is the only bilingual (English and Spanish) African American newspaper in New Jersey. Based at 25 South Westfield Avenue, Trenton, NJ 08618, the newspaper has been in continuous publication for over 35 years, covering local events, politics, and community issues relevant to Trenton's Black community. Kujichagulia remains the publisher and key principal. The Nubian News also produces the podcast 'On The Case,' featuring weekly interviews with community leaders every Thursday at 8 PM EST. The paper gained wider attention in 2020 when Kujichagulia organized a rally in support of Trenton councilwoman Robin Vaughn. The Nubian News Corporation operates the newspaper's current digital presence at thenubiannews.com. No digitized historical archive of print issues was found online.
