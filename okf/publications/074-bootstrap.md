## Historical notes

Bootstrap was a Black publication from Newark, NJ, active from approximately 1981 to 1983. No digitized copies or detailed historical records were found online. Newark had a vibrant Black publishing scene during this era; the Newark Public Library's New Jersey Information Center maintains extensive archives of local periodicals from this period, including publications like Grafrica (1981-1982). The Newark Public Library's Charles F. Cummings NJ Information Center would be the most likely repository for physical copies.
