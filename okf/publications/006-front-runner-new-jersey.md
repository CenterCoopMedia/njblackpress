## Mission statement

Front Runner New Jersey brings a new perspective on the people, events and issues making an impact on African-Americans in South Jersey and the nation.

## Key staff

President: Clyde Hughes.

## Historical notes

Front Runner New Jersey is a property of AC JosepH Media, LLC, founded by award-winning journalist Clyde Hughes who serves as president, CEO, and editor-in-chief. Based in Atlantic City, the publication covers African American and Latino communities in South Jersey, spanning Atlantic, Burlington, Camden, Cape May, Cumberland, Gloucester, and Salem counties. The outlet also launched a sister publication, Front Runner La Prensa, to cover the Latino community.
